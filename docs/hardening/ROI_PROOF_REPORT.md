## Executive Summary

The ROI Proof Engine quantifies the financial impact of the Zenith AI platform for each customer. Attribution is based on last-touch methodology using a $285 average production value per patient visit.

---

## Revenue Attribution

| Attribution Source | Calculation Method |
|---|---|
| Recall Revenue | Reactivated patients × $285 avg production |
| No-Show Reduction | Recovered appointments × $285 avg production |
| Treatment Upsell | Accepted treatment plans influenced by AI follow-up × fee |
| Lead Conversion | New patients converted via lead intelligence × $285 |

---

## Savings Attribution

| Savings Source | Calculation Method |
|---|---|
| Admin Time Saved | Hours reclaimed via automation × $25/hr blended rate |
| No-Show Cost Avoided | Prevented no-shows × $285 opportunity cost |

---

## Investment Tracking

- Subscription cost amortized monthly (plan price).
- Implementation cost amortized over 12 months.
- Total investment = subscription + (implementation ÷ 12).

---

## Methodology

- **Attribution model:** Last-touch — Zenith AI receives credit when platform action (recall message, lead follow-up, treatment reminder) precedes the patient visit.
- **Avg production value:** $285/patient visit (configurable per practice).
- **ROI formula:** `(Revenue Attributed + Savings − Investment) ÷ Investment × 100`
