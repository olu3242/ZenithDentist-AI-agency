## Executive Summary

The Dental Command Center delivers a unified practice intelligence dashboard. Health scoring, KPI tracking, trend analysis, and AI-generated recommendations are all operational.

---

## Health Score Dimensions

Five composite dimensions feed the overall practice health score:

| Dimension | Description |
|---|---|
| Revenue Health | Production vs. goal, collections rate, AR aging |
| Recall Performance | Recall rate, reappointment conversion, hygiene utilization |
| New Patient Conversion | Lead-to-appointment, appointment-to-case acceptance |
| Operational Efficiency | Schedule utilization, provider productivity, cancellation rate |
| Patient Satisfaction | NPS estimate, review velocity, complaint rate |

---

## AI Recommendations Engine

Recommendations generated in 4 categories:

- **Revenue** — treatment plan follow-up, fee schedule gaps
- **Recall** — patients overdue for hygiene with contact strategy
- **Conversion** — stalled leads and incomplete case presentations
- **Operations** — schedule holes, provider utilization alerts

Each recommendation carries a priority score and estimated production impact.

---

## KPI Tracking

6 core practice KPIs surfaced on the Command Center dashboard:

1. Monthly production vs. goal
2. New patient count (MTD / YTD)
3. Hygiene reappointment rate
4. Case acceptance rate
5. No-show / cancellation rate
6. Net Promoter Score (estimated)

---

## Trend Analysis — 3 Growth Vectors

| Vector | Signal |

| Patient Base Expansion | New patient acquisition vs. attrition |
| Treatment Mix | High-value procedure penetration rate |
